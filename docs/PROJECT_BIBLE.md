# ANP Adventures - Project Bible

## Project Overview

ANP Adventures is a Roblox multiplayer co-op adventure game inspired by real-world educational destinations in Thailand.

ANP stands for:

* Atom
* Neutron
* Proton

The game combines exploration, discovery, puzzle solving, teamwork and educational experiences through adventure gameplay.

Players are not students visiting a museum.

Players are explorers joining the ANP Adventure Team.

---

## Episode 1

Title:

The Lost Star Core

Location:

Space Inspirium

Story:

The Star Core that powers Space Inspirium has shattered into multiple fragments.

Players must work together with Atom, Neutron and Proton to recover the fragments and restore the facility.

---

## Target Audience

Primary:

* Children age 6-12

Secondary:

* Families
* Parents playing with children

---

## Core Design Pillars

### Adventure First

Gameplay must always be fun before educational.

### Discovery

Players should be rewarded for curiosity and exploration.

### Cooperation

Playing together should be encouraged.

### Solo Friendly

Every quest must be completable by a solo player.

### Learn Through Play

Educational content should be integrated into gameplay rather than presented as lessons.

---

## Supported Play Modes

* Solo
* Multiplayer Co-op

Players: 1-8

## Core Progression

Explorer Score

Players gain score from:

* Discovery
* Exploration
* Puzzle Solving

* Quest Completion
