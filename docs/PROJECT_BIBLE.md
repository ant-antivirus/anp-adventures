# ANP Adventures - Project Bible

## Project Overview

ANP Adventures is a Roblox multiplayer co-op adventure game inspired by real-world educational destinations in Thailand.

ANP stands for:

* Atom
* Neutron
* Proton

The game combines exploration, discovery, puzzle solving, teamwork and educational experiences through adventure gameplay.

Players are not students visiting a museum.

Players are explorers joining the ANP Adventure Team.

---

## Episode 1

Title:

The Lost Star Core

Location:

Space Inspirium

Story:

The Star Core system at Space Inspirium has become unstable, revealing that one Star Core Segment is missing its knowledge fragments.

Players work with Atom, Neutron and Proton to recover the Episode 1 fragments, restore `item_star_core_segment_01`, and stabilize Space Inspirium.

Episode 1 does not restore the full Star Core. It restores Star Core Segment 01 and reveals that future episodes must restore additional segments.

## Target Audience

Primary:

* Children age 6-12

Secondary:

* Families
* Parents playing with children

---

## Core Design Pillars

### Adventure First

Gameplay must always be fun before educational.

### Discovery

Players should be rewarded for curiosity and exploration.

### Cooperation

Playing together should be encouraged.

### Solo Friendly

Every quest must be completable by a solo player.

### Learn Through Play

Educational content should be integrated into gameplay rather than presented as lessons.

---

## Supported Play Modes

* Solo
* Multiplayer Co-op

Players: 1-8

## Core Progression

Explorer Score

Players gain score from:

* Discovery
* Exploration
* Puzzle Solving
* Teamwork
* Quest Completion

Persistent story progression includes:

* Episode 1 fragments
* Star Core Segment items
* Journal and lore unlocks

---

## Main Goal

Create memorable adventures that inspire children to explore real-world places.
