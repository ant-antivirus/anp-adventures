# ANP Adventures Monetization Policy

This document reserves long-term monetization rules. It does not authorize implementation of purchases, marketplace calls, premium payouts, products, passes, or paid systems in the current phase.

## Principle

Players should feel:

"I support this game because I love it."

Not:

"I must pay to keep up."

## Allowed Future Categories

- Cosmetics
- Titles
- Emotes
- Mount skins
- Pet skins
- Housing decorations
- Photo frames
- Campfire themes
- Family scrapbook themes
- Convenience that does not bypass required progression
- Optional memory-preserving presentation features

## Forbidden Categories

- Pay-to-win stats
- Damage/power boosts
- Exclusive gameplay advantage
- Required quest skips
- Required energy systems
- Forced spending gates
- Loot boxes that affect progression or advantage
- Paid access to mandatory progression
- Selling leaderboard advantage

## Implementation Guardrail

Monetization must support identity, memory, and expression. It must never sell required power, required progression, or competitive advantage.
